Bengaluru: In a sluggish growth scenario where firms are calibrating their freshers hiring plans,TCS COO N Ganapathy Subramaniam said the

company is on track to hire 40,000 campus recruits in the current financial year."We usually hire between 35,000 to 40,000 people and those plans are intact.There are no largescale layoffs.The way we've calibrated this is we;re working towards improving our own utilisation because we have a decent bench,"he said.
